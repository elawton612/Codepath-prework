There will be 8 rounds, each round the computer will add another color to the pattern.

To win, match the given sequence by clicking on the squares in the order they're highlighted.

You must repeat the full sequence correctly each time until you finish the sequence of 8 squares.

If you click the wrong square in the pattern that's a strike.

You have 3 strikes and the rounds will get faster each time.

Good Luck! :)
